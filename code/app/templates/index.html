About

Our mission is to save you time, reduce bias, and mitigate risk.

Time

If a deposition is a minimum of two hours, then necessarily a human must spend at least the same amount of time to read, reread, annotate and summarize that data. Our technology aims to help attorneys figure out which documents to look at first to get work done the most efficiently. If one case ends quickly, another can begin bringing a reputation for speed and final outcome.

Bias

Every person that reads a file, litigation document, etc. brings their own personal bias which may cloud their judgment. When a computer is allowed to sift through data it too necessarily has its own bias, but this bias is constant while simultaneously being malleable. If a data scientist notices bias in their model they can take steps to correct it. It is the versatility of the machine to reduce bias systematically and consistently that attracts lawyers to our product.

Risk

We refer to the risk we learned from our interview with a paralegal: spending time on the wrong witnesses, costing the client time in jail, allowing the client to suffer mental anguish as they await their lawyers, and the risk of putting the wrong witness on the stand to testify. Our product is meant to guide lawyers through mountains of paperwork, testimonies, and depositions in order to find their golden ticket to success.
